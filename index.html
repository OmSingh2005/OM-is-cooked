Ingredients:

  1. Marination
    • 1/2 cup hung Curd
    • 1/4 cup BBQ sauce
    • 2 tbsp Ginger garlic paste
    • 1x masoom smile🙂 for binding
    • And थोरा सा Chef का प्यार
  2. Seasoning 🧂
    • Grounded Flirt chilli 🌶 (ढेर सारी 😎)
    • Salt pepper
    • MDH kebab mix
  3. Base Meat 🥺
    • 2x meaty boneless arms (1kg)
    • 2x Chunky cheeks with dimple (150g)
    • Chhotu ka brain 🧠 (500g)
  1. SO you know that, you are the cutie❤️
  2. Mix all marination ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Add the seasoning and mix well.
  4. Combine the base meat with the marination mixture.
  5. Let it marinate for at least 2 hours in the refrigerator.
  6. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  7. Grill the kebabs until fully cooked, about 10-15 minutes.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
