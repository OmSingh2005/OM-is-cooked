Delicious Kebab Recipe

This is a delicious kebab recipe that you can make at home with simple ingredients.

Ingredients:

  1. Marination
    • 1/2 cup hung Curd
    • 1/4 cup BBQ sauce
    • 2 tbsp Ginger garlic paste
    • 1x masoom smile🙂 for binding
    • And थोरा सा Chef का प्यार
  2. Seasoning 🧂
    • Grounded Flirt chilli 🌶 (ढेर सारी 😎)
    • Salt pepper
    • MDH kebab mix
  3. Base Meat 🥺
    • 2x meaty boneless arms (1kg)
    • 2x Chunky cheeks with dimple (150g)
    • Chhotu ka brain 🧠 (500g)
