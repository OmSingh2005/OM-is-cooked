Delicious Kebab Recipe

This is a delicious kebab recipe that you can make at home with simple ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground meat (beef or lamb)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, mix together the ground meat, onion, garlic, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  2. Form the mixture into small patties or skewer onto metal or soaked wooden skewers.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Grill the kebabs for 4-5 minutes per side, or until cooked through and lightly charred.
  5. Remove from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.
