Instructions:

  1. Marinate the meat in a mixture of 1/2 cup hung curd, 1/4 cup BBQ sauce, 2 tbsp ginger garlic paste, and add a touch of love from the chef.
  2. Season the marinated meat with grounded flirt chili, salt, pepper, and a sprinkle of MDH kebab mix.
  3. Skewer the marinated meat onto metal or soaked wooden skewers, alternating with chunky cheeks with dimples and chhotu ka brain.
  4. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  5. Grill the kebabs for 4-5 minutes per side, or until cooked through and lightly charred.
  6. Remove from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving with a side of masoom smile.
